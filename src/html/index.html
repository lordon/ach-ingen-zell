-berg, -dorf, -ing, -itz

A visual exploration of the spatial patterns in the endings of Austrian Steiermark's town and village names.

Darker colors indicate a higher proportion of place names with the respective suffix in a geographic cell. Hover over a cell for a while to see a tooltip with the place names.

Inspired and forked from Moritz Stefaner's -ach, -ingen, -zell http://github.com/moritzstefaner/ach-ingen-zell

Source: Bundesanstalt Statistik Austria 2017

Raphael Saalmann, 2017
